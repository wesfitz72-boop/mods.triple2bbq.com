🔧 How to Install a PID Controller on a Southern Pride Smoker

If you're tired of temperature swings, a PID controller is the ultimate upgrade. Here’s how to install one on your Southern Pride smoker — and make it cook like a dream.

🧰 What You’ll Need:

  • Inkbird ITC-1000 PID Controller
  • High Temp Thermocouple Probe
  • Heat-resistant wire
  • Wire nuts, terminal block, and a screwdriver

🔌 Step-by-Step Install:

  1. Unplug your smoker and open the control box.
  2. Mount the PID controller in the panel (or in a project box externally).
  3. Wire the controller per the included diagram:
    Pin 1/2 - Power, Pin 3/4 - Load, Pin 7/8 - Thermocouple
  4. Install the thermocouple probe inside the smoker chamber.
  5. Test it on low heat first. Adjust PID values as needed.

💡 Pro Tip:

Set your PID with a short cycle time for quicker adjustments. Southern Pride units typically stabilize at around 225°F with a 5-second cycle.
